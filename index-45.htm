Welcome to CG!

RandomCG aOwner

Once again after months on hiatus, the Owners and Staff team of Celestial Gaming have returned to bring you this spectacular server. With constant uptime, amazing features, the world's greatest community and routine updates, we're proud to announce that we aspire to shatter all records made by the older version of this server!

This means we're going to be putting in even more work to listen to player input, to take and apply advice, to be more understanding and finally, to ensure every single one of our players has an enjoyable experience during their time on CG.

Please make sure to report any bugs to a Staff member, and don't hesitate to direct message one of us on Discord if something bothers you. There are still some plugins that need to be updated to the 1.13 version of Minecraft, but we've added almost all of the plugins that you loved from the old server. With your constant support, we'll be able to break limits that we could've never even imagined to back in the old days of this server, so for that.. thank you sincerely. It's incredible how far we've come.

Additonally, the new Celestial Donator rank is finally available for purchase!

Welcome back, everyone. We wish you the best during your time, because without you... our little family here wouldn't be the same.
